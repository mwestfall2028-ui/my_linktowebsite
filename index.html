Dolphins are highley intelligent and playful marine animals. They are warm blooded and air breathing. They also use echolocation for hunting.

5 basic facts about dolphins

(JIM SCHULTZ/CHICAGO ZOOLOGICAL SOCIETY)

  1. There are currently 42 species of dolphins.
  2. Dolphins eat fish, squid and crustaceans.
  3. Lifespan varies from around 20 years in the smaller dolphin species to 80 years or more for larger dolphins .
  4. A dolphin pregnancy can last between 9 and 16 months.
  5. Dolphins are marine mammals. They must surface to breathe air and give birth to live young.
